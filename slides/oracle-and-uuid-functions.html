Oracle UUID Functions

  • SYS_GUID(): Generate a new, unique 128-bit value
  • RAWTOHEX(): Convert binary to hexidecimal digits
  • HEXTORAW(): Convert hexidecimal digits to binary
  • SYS_GUID() returns a RAW(16), not a GUID!
  • RAWTOHEX() does NOT provide dashes, only hexidecimal digits.
  • Cannot compare hex to raw. Use rawtohex() or hextoraw() first.
